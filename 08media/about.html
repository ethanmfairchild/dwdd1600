About Our Page

Welcome to The Lineup, a community built by surfers, for surfers. Whether you're chasing the perfect wave, looking for the latest surf edit, or searching for expert tips on gear and technique, we've got you covered. Connect with fellow wave riders, stay updated on competition dates, and dive into a world of surf knowledge - all in one place. This is more than just a website; it's a hub for the surf culture we all love. See you in the lineup!

Page Features:

  • A collection of our favorite surf edits, videos, and films
  • Details and dates for surf competions around the world
  • Information on board shape, fins, liters and anything else you need to know to get in the water
  • Tips and reccomendations for the top brands for boards, boardshorts, wetsuits, and surfwear
