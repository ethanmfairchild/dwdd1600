Terms to know before you get into it:

Stoke
A term used to describe the pure excitement and joy that comes from surfing. If someone is "stoked," they're feeling amped up, happy, and energized-whether it's from catching an epic wave or just being out in the water.
Getting Barrelled
This refers to riding inside the hollow part of a wave, also known as the tube. It's one of the most sought-after experiences in surfing, where the wave curls over the surfer, creating a tunnel-like effect.
It's Pumping
A phrase used when the waves are really good-big, clean, and consistent. When someone says, "It's pumping," it means the surf conditions are on fire, and it's time to grab your board and hit the water.
